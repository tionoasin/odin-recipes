Hashbrown cassarole

Description

There's no need for complexity, this dish is a cassarole made exclusively from Hashbrown, alright, not quite.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Hashbrowns: You'll want to thaw these.
  • Canned Soup: Cream of some sort sort, be it chicken or mushroom.
  • Cheddar: or anything cheese like that'll melt
  • Sour Cream: 8 ozs needed, vegan is just as well
  • Butter: 1 stick or 1/2-3/4 cups of a substitute
  • Onion: 1/2 cup chopped
  • Seasonings: Salt and ground black pepper for starts. Nutritional Yeast and cumin to add some pungent cheesiness. Get creative with it!
  • Cornflakes: A cup crushed to top

    • I've actually never made this recipe, I'm copying it from All Recipes for the sake of learning HTML but I imagine it's delicious

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 350 and gather ingredients
  2. Mix all except cornflakes and transfer to 9x13 caserole dish
  3. Sprinkle cornflakes on top and drizzle a little extra oil/fat that wasn't actually accounted for in the ingredients
  4. Bake 1-1 1/2 hours, look for the sauce to be bubbling and the top to be golden brown
  5. It's done, do with it what you will
