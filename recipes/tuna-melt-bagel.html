NutButter Toast

Description

Once again, Delicious and simple protein in the morning, or even for lunch! Tuna Salad on cheese on bagel. Open faced or it'll all squeeze out.

Ingredients

  • A Bagel: I use a plain bagel
  • 2 Slices of Cheese of Choice: I prefer sliced cheese to cover the hole in the bagel. I use American as it's kept stocked in my fridge. Creativity welcomed!
  • A Can of Tuna: You can split one can between the two halves. I give a little bit of it to my cats and still have enough leftover.
  • Mayo: A good blob of it, the more the less the tuna will hold it's structure.
  • Spices: I use basil, plenty of salt, pepper, a little squirt of sriracha, and a little squirt of lemon juice but all of this is optional and you can be creative here.

Steps

  1. Open Tuna and add to a bowl, mix with mayo and spices.
  2. Toast bagel
  3. Place slice of cheese on each face of the bagel
  4. Glob the Tuna Salad on top of the cheese
  5. Microwave if you want, or each it as is
