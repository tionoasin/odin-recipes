NutButter Toast

Description

Delicious and simple protein in the morning, it's just Nutbutter spread across toast. I prefer Sunflower Butter.

Ingredients

  • Sliced Bread
  • Nut Butter of Choice: Peanut Butter is traditional but often deadly. Sunflower Butter is delcious and expensive. Almond butter is delicious and a little less expensive.

    • Om nom nom nom, so good!

Steps

  1. Toast bread, toaster is fine, if you wanna be fancy you could like butter and fry it or something but simplicity is key here
  2. Lay toast on surface
  3. Mix Nut Butter if needed and gather on knife
  4. Spread Nut Butter on toast
  5. It's done, I'd recommend eating it!
